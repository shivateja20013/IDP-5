Vizag

Visakhapatnam, often referred to as Vizag, is a prominent coastal city in the state of Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its beautiful beaches, natural beauty, and a mix of modern and historical attractions.

City Information

Visakhapatnam is a major port city on the Bay of Bengal and is often called the "Jewel of the East Coast." It is the largest city in Andhra Pradesh after the state capital, Amaravati.
Visakhapatnam is a hub for trade, industry, and tourism.

Tourist Spots

Rushikonda Beach

Rushikonda Beach is a pristine and popular beach known for its golden sands and clear waters.
It's a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports like surfing.

RK Beach (Ramakrishna Beach)

RK Beach is a famous urban beach known for its long coastline and scenic beauty.
It's a popular spot for evening walks, and you can find numerous food stalls and street vendors offering local snacks and souvenirs

INS Kursura Submarine Museum

This museum is housed in a real decommissioned submarine, the INS Kursura.
Visitors can explore the submarine's interior and learn about India's maritime history and naval operations.

Borra Caves

While not in Vizag itself, the Borra Caves are a few hours' drive away and are worth a visit.
These natural limestone caves are famous for their unique formations and ancient tribal folklore

Kailasagiri:

Kailasagiri is a hill park offering panoramic views of the city and the Bay of Bengal.
It has a massive statue of Lord Shiva and Parvati and is a popular spot for picnics and recreational activities.

Visakhapatnam is a fantastic destination for beach lovers, history enthusiasts, and nature admirers. The city's blend of natural beauty, modern amenities, and cultural attractions make it a popular tourist spot in Andhra Pradesh
