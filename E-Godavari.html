City Information

East Godavari is one of the 13 districts in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, and it's known for its cultural heritage, lush green landscapes, and the famous Godavari River.
East Godavari district is located in the eastern part of Andhra Pradesh and is one of the largest districts in the state.

Tourist Spots

Rajahmundry

Rajahmundry is the largest city in the East Godavari district and is often considered the cultural and administrative hub of the region.
The city is situated on the banks of the Godavari River and offers picturesque views and a peaceful environment.

Godavari River Cruises

The Godavari River is one of the largest rivers in India, and boat cruises along the river are a popular way to explore the region's beauty.
Cruises often include visits to nearby villages and provide an opportunity to enjoy the serene river surroundings.

Papi Kondalu

Papi Kondalu is a range of hills located in the Godavari River basin.
It's a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts, offering opportunities for trekking, boating, and enjoying the natural beauty.

Konaseema

Konaseema is a picturesque region in East Godavari known for its lush green coconut groves, backwaters, and serene landscapes.
It's a perfect place to relax and enjoy the tranquility of the countryside.

East Godavari district offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and religious significance. The Godavari River and its surroundings are a major attraction for tourists, providing opportunities for relaxation and adventure.
